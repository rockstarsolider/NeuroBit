Manifesto

This course is offered to teenagers and young adults aged 15 and above who are interested in software engineering and technology.

Upon completion, participants can work in remote software teams and, if they wish, in-person.

Motivation and perseverance are more important than academic background, which is not a prerequisite.

Access to a suitable computer for coding, with a webcam and sufficient internet speed for online sessions, is required.

Because much of the course material is in English, a basic proficiency in reading, listening, and comprehension of the language is necessary.
